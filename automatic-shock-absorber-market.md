<p><ul><li>Overflow Hole Shock Absorber</li><li>Annular Hole Shock Absorber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic shock absorber market features various types, including overflow hole and annular hole shock absorbers. Overflow hole shock absorbers utilize a single outlet for fluid displacement, allowing for quick response and adaptability during dynamic conditions. Annular hole shock absorbers, on the other hand, employ multiple smaller openings around a central core, enhancing stability and control by distributing forces evenly. Both designs aim to enhance ride comfort and vehicle performance by efficiently managing shock waves and vibrations during motion.</p></p>
