<p><p>Circulating Fluid Bed (CFB) boilers are categorized into three market types based on pressure and efficiency. Subcritical CFB boilers operate below the critical pressure point, offering lower efficiency and higher emissions. Supercritical CFB boilers operate above this point, achieving higher efficiency and reduced emissions, making them more environmentally friendly. Ultra-supercritical CFB boilers push efficiency even further by operating at higher temperatures and pressures, optimizing thermal performance and minimizing fuel consumption, thus representing the leading edge of CFB technology.</p></p>
