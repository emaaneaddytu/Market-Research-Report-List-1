<p><p>Manual pull stations are critical components in fire alarm systems, designed to allow individuals to signal an emergency. The market is primarily divided into two types: single action and dual action. Single action pull stations require one simple pull to activate the alarm, making them user-friendly. In contrast, dual action models necessitate two distinct actions, such as lifting a cover and pulling a lever, providing an extra layer of prevention against false alarms. Both types have unique applications based on safety needs.</p></p>
