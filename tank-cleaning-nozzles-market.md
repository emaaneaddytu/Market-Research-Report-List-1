<p><strong>The Tank Cleaning Nozzles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Nozzles</li><li>Spray Balls</li><li>Rotary Spray Balls</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tank cleaning nozzles market comprises three main types: static nozzles, spray balls, and rotary spray balls. Static nozzles remain fixed and direct high-pressure streams to clean tank surfaces, making them efficient for smaller tanks. Spray balls rotate in a fixed position, providing uniform coverage through a set pattern, ideal for medium to large tanks. Rotary spray balls, equipped with multiple nozzles, spin as they operate, ensuring superior cleaning efficiency for larger tanks by reaching more complex geometries and surfaces.</p></p>
