<p><ul><li>Bottom Vivration</li><li>Side Viration</li><li>Top Viration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic welding transducers are categorized into three main types based on their vibration modes: bottom vibration, side vibration, and top vibration. </p><p>Bottom vibration transducers are designed for applications that require the welding tip to be anchored below the workpieces, providing stability. Side vibration transducers allow for lateral energy transfer, making them suitable for joining irregular shapes. Top vibration transducers generate ultrasonic waves from above, ideal for processing materials with varying thicknesses or geometries, ensuring effective bonding in diverse applications.</p></p>
